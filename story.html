WHERE THE WILD THINGS WED

We met at an art walk in Seattle, literally bumping into each other
We started hiking together,a lot. We both love the PNW, the
mountains, the coast,everything. We'd be out on trails,just
hanging out,talking around a fire. It clicked.We were on the
same wavelength. One time, we were hiking and I complained
about my boots and Jenny just stopped and started tying my
laces for me. That's when I knew. It wasn't some grand gesture. It
was just...us.

We’ve built a life together. It’s chill, it’s real. We love low-key
adventures, but also appreciate good wine with friends. From
those first hikes to figuring out life together under countless
starry skies, we've alwaysfound our best moments in the wild.

It feels right to celebrate surrounded by the people who
get us. We're stoked to start this next chapter with you.

Onward and upward to the next adventure!
